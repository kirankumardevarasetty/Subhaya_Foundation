ASHRAYA NIVAS

As our 1000th Project we have decided to start our own Centres which accomodates Unattended elders and Kids, we named it ASHRAYA NIVAS at Khammam.

Process:
  • As of now our Ashraya Nivas will accomodate 15 Unattended elders and we will take care them as our own family members.
  • We will be providing them Accomodation, Food and Home with all the facilities like Television, Security, CC Cameras, Well maintained       beds, Washing Machines, Water Dispensers, Water Purifiers etc
  • Per Day Meals costing us Rs 3500 per day which includes salaries to CHefs, Maids, Care Taker, To Sponsor Meals or Groceries
           Please send whatsapp message to 6303251670.

NITYA AAHAR

Under Nitya Aahar Program, From Feb 19 - 2021 we are Daily Providing 100 Free MEALS to Patients attendants at Women and Child Wing, GOVERNMENT HOSPITAL KHAMMAM.

Process:
  • Daily We Cook food at our Kitchen and we recently bought our own Maruthi EECO Van for Carrying Prepared food to the hospital.
  • Our Volunteer Team Serving Rice, Curry, Fry, Chutney and Curd and Occassionally Fruits and Sweets.
  • Per Day Meals costing us Rs 5200 per day which includes salaries to CHefs, Maids, Drivers, Volunteers, To Sponsor Meals or Groceries
          Please send whatsapp message to 6303251670.

VIDVATH

Under VIDVATH Program,a SCHOLARSHIP worth INR 5000 will be given to the students from poor financial background especially those from orphanages or with Single Parent.

Process:
  • VIDVATH Application need to be filled and Required documents need to be sent
  • After Application Received our Volunteers will do Enquiries
  • Based on Feedback Scholarship Amount will be transferred directly to the Educational Institutes

ASHRAYA

Under ASHRAYA Program, PENSION will be given to the Old people above 65 Yrs or people with Permanent Disabilities.

Process:
  • ASHRAYA Application needs to be filled and Required documents need to be sent.
  • After Receiving the Application our Volunteers team will do Enquiries.
  • Based on Feedback Pension Amount will be transferred directly to the Pensioner every month.

SAHAY

Under SAHAY Program SUPPORT will be given to the Orphanage/Oldage Homes/People in Need by providing them with Groceries, essentials like water dispensaries, beds, furniture, etc.conducting Medical Camps, offering timely medical Support and doctor consultations.

Process:
  • Our Volunteers will visit Orphanage/Oldage Homes.
  • Volunteers will have personal interaction with the Caretakers and inmates.
  • Based on Feedback support will be provided.

APATBANDH

Under APATBANDH Program FUNDS will be given to the Patients in need of emergency and immediate health care attention.

Process:
  • APATBANDH Application need to be filled and Required documents need to be sent.
  • After Application Received our Volunteers team will do Enquiries and will be sent to our panel of doctors.
  • Based on Feedback Support will be provided, Social media campaign also will be done on case to case basis

PADHAYI

Under our PADHAYI Program SUPPORT will be given to the Government Schools by providing them facilities Like Water Dispensers, Computers, Lab equipment, Shoes and Socks, Library Facilities, Lunch Plates, stationary, Sanitary Napkins.

Process:
  • Our Volunteers will visit the Government Schools.
  • Volunteers will have personal interaction with the Teachers and Students.
  • Based on Feedback support will be provided.
